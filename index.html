About Us

New Venture, Proven Expertise

We are delighted to introduce our new venture, founded by Mrs. Bharathi Sivakumar and Mr. Achyuth Sivakumar. Our factory is equipped with the latest state-of-the-art automated machinery, designed to ensure precision, efficiency, and consistency in every garment we produce.

Specializing in high-quality formal trousers, casual chinos, rugged cargo pants, and premium denim, we aim to cater to both domestic and international markets with superior quality products that reflect style, comfort, and durability. Built on the pillars of innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, our mission is to set new standards in trouser manufacturing through modern technology, skilled craftsmanship, and a deep commitment to excellence.

Capabilities

Technological Edge

  • Machinery

Segment-Specific Machinery

Specialized machines for both formal and casual trousers. Precision pleating, waistband attachers, blind stitch hemmers for formals. Heavy-duty flatbed sewing, bartackers, and automatic rivet setters for casuals/denim.

  • CAD/CAM

Pattern Adaptability

Flexible production utilizing CAD/CAM systems to switch between styles and fits. Seamless design-to-production transition for everything from slim-fit poly-viscose to stretch denim cargos.

  • Materials

Material Handling Expertise

Deep experience with diverse fabrics: poly-viscose, cotton twill, denim, lycra blends, and stretch gabardine. Systems trained for fabric-specific tensions, shrinkages, and finishing.

Leadership

Our Visionaries

Mrs. Bharathi Sivakumar

Founder

Mrs. Bharathi Sivakumar is the founder of this new-age apparel venture. With over a decade of hands-on experience in the garment industry, she has developed strong expertise in design, quality, and understanding market needs. Her career includes working with buying houses, international clients, and production teams, giving her well-rounded knowledge of the entire manufacturing process.

Her passion for clothing, along with her technical skills and industry insights, led her to establish a small but well-equipped unit that values efficiency, quality, and sustainable practices. Through this venture, she aims to create garments that combine style, comfort, and durability while also supporting employment opportunities and encouraging women’s participation in the industry.

Mr. Achyuth Sivakumar

Director

Mr. Achyuth Sivakumar, Director of Texloom Pvt Ltd, is a dynamic young leader with a strong academic foundation and a forward-looking vision for the apparel manufacturing industry. He holds a Master’s degree in Materials Science and Engineering from the University of Birmingham, UK, and a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from SRM University, Chennai.

With expertise spanning manufacturing processes, advanced materials, quality control, and creative design, Achyuth brings a rare combination of technical precision and innovative thinking to Texloom. His vision is to drive innovation through automation, foster continuous improvement, and build a brand that reflects quality, trust, and long-term value for clients worldwide.

Manufacturing Excellence

Industry 4.0 Integration

Our manufacturing facility is built with a future-ready infrastructure that reflects the cutting edge of global garment production standards. The unit is equipped with high-precision, fully-automated machines for every stage of trouser production.

A key differentiator of our facility is its integration with Industry 4.0 and IoT technologies (JANET & JUKI). Our smart production floor is supported by a Manufacturing Execution System (MES) that enables dynamic scheduling, line balancing, and quality control tracking.
